Política de Privacidad

Última actualización: 2026-03-26

ArteriaFit respects your privacy. This app stores all data locally on your device.

We collect minimal data necessary for the app functionality:

  • Blood pressure readings (stored locally)
  • Session data for the 7-day BP protocol
  • User preferences (theme, notifications)

No data is sent to external servers without your consent.

For more information, contact: privacy@arteriafit.com
